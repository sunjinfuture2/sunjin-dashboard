GPUaaS Annual Revenue

Σ(rack count × GPUs per rack × 8,760 h × utilization × GPU hourly rate)

Colocation Annual Revenue

IT power (kW) × colocation rate × 12 months + total power (kW) × 8,760 h × utilization × electricity resale rate

IT Equipment CAPEX

Σ(rack count × rack price (USD) × FX)

Electricity Cost

total power (kW) × basic charge × 12 + used IT power (kW) × PUE × 8,760 h × utilization × energy rate
